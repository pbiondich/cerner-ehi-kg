# PM_CONSUMER_ACTIVITY

> Stores activity data for various Person Management consumer activities and transactions.

**Description:** Person Management Consumer Activity  
**Table type:** ACTIVITY  
**Primary key:** _(not published — see note)_  
**Columns:** 16
